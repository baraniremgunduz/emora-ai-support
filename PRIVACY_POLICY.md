## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Emora AI ("we," "our," "us," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our App. The types of information we collect include:

### 2.1 Account Information
- **Email Address:** Required for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Password:** Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt hashing)
- **Name/Username:** Used for personalization and profile display
- **Profile Photo (Optional):** Stored securely in our cloud storage

### 2.2 Chat Data
- **Chat Messages:** All conversations between you and the AI assistant
- **Chat History:** Your chat sessions, including titles and timestamps
- **Chat Preferences:** Your settings and preferences for chat interactions

### 2.3 Device Information
- **Device Model:** To optimize app performance and compatibility
- **Operating System Version:** For compatibility testing and updates
- **App Version:** For debugging, support, and feature development
- **Device ID:** For push notification delivery and analytics
- **Push Notification Tokens:** To send you notifications (with your consent)

### 2.4 Usage Data
- **App Usage Statistics:** Feature usage, screen views, and interaction patterns
- **Analytics Events:** User behavior data to improve app functionality
- **Performance Metrics:** App launch times, response times, and stability metrics
- **Error Reports:** Crash logs and error diagnostics to improve app stability

### 2.5 Premium Subscription Information
- **Subscription Status:** To enable/disable premium features
- **Purchase History:** Transaction records (processed securely through Apple App Store)
- **Payment Information:** Handled exclusively by Apple App Store - we do not store payment details

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure
- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL encryption
- Sensitive data (passwords) is encrypted at rest using bcrypt hashing
- Data is stored in secure, access-controlled databases

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data - only authorized personnel
- **Regular Security Audits:** We regularly review and update our security practices
- **Secure Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- **Data Backup:** Regular backups to prevent data loss
- **Incident Response:** Procedures in place to respond to security incidents

### 4.3 Third-Party Services
We use the following trusted third-party services:
- **Supabase:** Database, authentication, and cloud storage services

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We ensure all third-party services comply with applicable data protection laws.
